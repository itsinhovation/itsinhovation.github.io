About

I am a Ph.D. student at the Trustworthy AI Lab, advised by Prof. Jongheon Jeong at Korea University. My research interests are in AI security, with a particular focus on AI-generated content detection. I aim to study both known and emerging security challenges in widely used foundation models and contribute to making them safer and more trustworthy in practice.
